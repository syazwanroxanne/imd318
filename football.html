About My Basketball Hobby

Here's where I share about my first hobby, which is football. I come from a family that loves sports, and we usually work out every day. My brother and sister are gym enthusiasts who hit the gym daily to maintain a healthy lifestyle. Unlike them, I'm the one in the family who enjoys rough sports like football. The main reason I love football so much is because of my idol, Cristiano Ronaldo. Despite turning 40 next year, he still plays exceptionally well. He was even more phenomenal during his prime, earning the admiration of many football fans. Cristiano Ronaldo has won numerous prestigious awards that many football players can only dream of, such as the FIFA World Player of the Year 2009, The Best FIFA Men's Player 2017, UEFA Men's Player of the Year Award 2014, FIFA Ballon d'Or in 2015, 2014, and 2017, FIFA Puskás Award 2009, FIFA FIFPro World XI in 2022, 2020, and 2019, and the Premier League Golden Boot in 2007. In conclusion, Cristiano Ronaldo deserves to be regarded as the best football player because he worked hard to rise from obscurity to his current status.
