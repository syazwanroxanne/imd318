About My Basketball Hobby

So this is where I share about my other hobby, which is basketball. My family loves this sport and they really support me to play basketball. My brother and sister are gym enthusiasts who hit the gym daily to maintain a healthy lifestyle, so they wanted me to play basketball because this sport can make me taller and increase my height durability. Unlike them, I'm the one in the family who enjoys rough sports like basketball. The main reason I love basketball so much is because of my idol, LeBron James. Despite turning 40 next year, he still plays exceptionally well. He was even more phenomenal during his prime, earning the admiration of many basketball fans. LeBron James has won numerous prestigious awards that many basketball players can only dream of, such as the NBA MVP Award in 2009, 2010, 2012, and 2013; the NBA Finals MVP Award in 2012, 2013, 2016, and 2020; the NBA All-Star Game MVP in 2006, 2008, and 2018; and the NBA Scoring Title in 2008. In conclusion, LeBron James deserves to be regarded as the best basketball player because he worked hard to rise from obscurity to his current status.
